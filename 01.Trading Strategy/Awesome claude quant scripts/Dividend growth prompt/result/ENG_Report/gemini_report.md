### 1-2. Key Investment Theses

1.  **The 'Duration-Premium' of Cash Flows:** High-conviction picks like **UNH** and **V** maintain exceptionally low payout ratios (sub-30%). This provides a "duration buffer," meaning they can sustain double-digit dividend growth for a decade solely from current free cash flow, regardless of short-term earnings volatility.
2.  **Monetizing the AI Cycle via Dividends:** **MSFT** and **AVGO** represent the intersection of high-growth tech and capital discipline. As AI infrastructure reaches the "recurring revenue" stage, these firms are converting cloud growth into tangible shareholder yield.
3.  **Capital Efficiency in Cyclical Sectors:** **PH** and **EOG** demonstrate elite ROIC (Return on Invested Capital). Their ability to maintain a pristine balance sheet while returning capital through base-and-variable dividend structures makes them ideal hedges against inflation.
