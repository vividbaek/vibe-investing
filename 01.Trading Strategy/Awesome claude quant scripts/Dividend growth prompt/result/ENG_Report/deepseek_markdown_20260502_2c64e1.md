### 1. Costco Wholesale (COST) – Consumer Staples
**Investment Theses:**
- **Subscription-like revenue model:** 73%+ membership renewal rate globally ensures resilient cash flows; 5-year revenue CAGR 9.3%, allowing double-digit dividend growth while retaining 74% of earnings.
- **Dividend growth narrative:** 19 consecutive annual increases, 5-year DPS CAGR 10.2% with payout ratio just 26% – FCF dividend coverage >3.5x.
- **Valuation support:** Forward P/E 38x vs. 5-year average 36x, supported by scarcity premium for reliable compounders; institutional ownership up 2.3% in latest 13F filings.

**Risk Factors:**
- Consumer spending sensitivity in a recession; gasoline profitability tied to gross margin.
- Margin compression if membership fee increases face political/regulatory pushback.

**12-Month Outlook:**
- Target dividend return: +12% (to $6.54/share annualized)  
- Capital return target: +8% (price $1,128)  
- Combined total return target: ~8.6%  
- Stop-loss threshold: $940 (-10% from current)

The strategy suits total return-oriented investors willing to accept lower current income in exchange for compounding dividend growth and capital appreciation.
