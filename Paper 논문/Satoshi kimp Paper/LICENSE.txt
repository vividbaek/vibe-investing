NOTE ON DATA
---------------------------------------------------------------

All datasets in /data are *synthetic* and calibrated to publicly reported
stylized facts. They do not contain real trading data from Bithumb, Binance,
OKX, or Bybit; the random seed (20260513) makes the series exactly
reproducible. Statistical results in the paper are properties of these
synthetic series, not empirical findings on real markets. See Appendix A
of the paper for the full calibration mechanism and moment-matching against
the literature.
